Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Cloud Resource?

A virtual machine such as AWS EC2, or a database such as Google Cloud SQL.

What is an Environment?

An Environment is a collection of resources. For example your "Shared Services" environment may include virtual machines and databases that are used by your CI/CD system.

What is a Schedule?

You define a schedule to control that when an individual resource or an environment (a collection of resources) must be turned on or off.

What is AIPO?

AI-Powered Optimizer is our artifial intelligence robot. It analyzes your usage data and comes up with optimized schedules that can maximise your savings.
